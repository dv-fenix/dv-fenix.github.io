Biography

Hi! I am a senior at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee, majoring in Electronics and Communications Engineering. I am currently working as a Research Assistant advised by Prof. Jonghyun Choi at the GIST Computer Vision Lab.

Previously, I was a Mitacs Globalink Research Intern advised by Prof. Fatemeh H. Fard at the University of British Columbia (Summer 2021) where I led a collaborative effort to develop efficient but accurate language models for software engineering tasks. I have also worked as a Research and Development Intern at Pando Labs (Fall 2020). We developed novel deep reinforcement learning solutions for operations research problems in the supply chain industry.

At IIT Roorkee, I am a core member of a Deep Learning research centric reading group named the Vision and Language Group, and the IEEE Student Chapter, IIT Roorkee. My broad research interests are in Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning.
