Biography

Hi! I am a rising senior at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee, majoring in Electronics and Communications Engineering. I am currently interested in Deep Learning, with particular applications in Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision.

I am currently working as a MITACS Globalink Research Intern at Prof. Fatemeh H. Fard’s lab at UBC. I am also working as a Summer Intern at Prof. Jonghyun Choi’s lab at GIST.

At IIT Roorkee, I am a core member of a Deep Learning research centric reading group named the Vision and Language Group, and the IEEE Student Chapter, IIT Roorkee. I have also served as the Chief News Coordinator of the official student media body of the university - Watch Out!.

I worked as a Research and Development Intern at Pando Labs - PandoCorp PLC during the fall of 2020. In the past, I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Saurabh Khanna, Prof. Raksha Sharma and Prof. Balasubramanian Raman at IIT Roorkee.

In my free time, I love reading about economics and human psychology. My other interests include playing football, squash and the guitar.
